Tree of Life

Hover or tap a sephirah or path. The 10 nodes are the sephiroth (one per minor card by suit). The 22 paths between them are the major arcana, each carrying a Hebrew letter.

36 Decans

Each 10° wedge of the zodiac corresponds to one minor pip card (2 through 10). Hover or tap a wedge to see the planetary ruler and the GD title.
